Care Notes

Designed for calm care, bright windows, and patient observation.

Sunflowers are generous when their basics are steady: strong light, deep watering, open soil, and enough space for roots to breathe. Treat the care rhythm like a slow animation: small, consistent inputs become dramatic growth.

Light

Choose the brightest position available. Rotate containers weekly for a straighter stem.

Water

Water deeply when the top layer of soil dries, then let excess drain completely.

Soil

Use loose, nutrient-rich soil with enough structure to support rapid root growth.

Space

Give each plant room so leaves can open without trapping moisture against the stem.

Details

Every surface has a job: catch light, move water, invite pollinators.

Leaves
